The Horror Hotel

Who was responsible? H.H.Holmes


Description: H.H.Holmes trapped women in his hotel and killed them.
There were stairs that lead to nowhere, trap doors, a human sized stove to burn people in and a home made gas chamber.
Kill Count: Holmes boasted about killing 27 people. However, some believe that he killed as little as 9 and as many as 200. He did not spare children.
Location: Chicago
When: The early 1900's
